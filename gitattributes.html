The Life and Career of Kevin Durant

Kevin Durant is one of the most dominant and versatile players in NBA history. Born in 1988 in Washington D.C., Durant has established himself as a scoring machine, having won multiple championships and MVPs in a career full of glory and controversy.

Early Life and College Career

Kevin Durant grew up in Seat Pleasant, Maryland, and quickly became a high school basketball sensation. He spent just one year at the University of Texas, dominating college hoops and earning National Player of the Year honors before declaring for the 2007 NBA draft.

NBA Stardom and Championships

Durant was drafted second overall by the Seattle Supersonics in 2007, and after the team's relocation, he became the face of the Oklahoma City Thunder. In 2014, he earned his first MVP and later joined the Golden State Warriors in 2016, where he won NBA championships and two Finals MVPs.

Career Achievements

  1. 2× NBA Champion (2017, 2018)
  2. 2× NBA Finals MVP (2017, 2018)
  3. NBA Most Valuable Player (2014)
  4. 13× NBA All-Star
  5. 4× NBA Scoring Champion
